We Are Udaan

The main motive of our club is to promote the entrepreneurship in the department and university, it also focuses on the development of the relevant 21st century skills and corporate like exposures through various events (online/offline) both, webinars and entrepreneur talks.

What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to launch and grow your startup

Building relevant skill in students

We organize events from different fields so that students can get a vast and varied amount of knowledge through us. And with our events they can develop relevant skills for their academics and startups.

Exposure of corporate industry

Through our events we provide an opportunity to students for an Exposure to corporate industry like how it works and what is needed and required to enter in it.

Incubation of startups with GEU-TBI

We help students in the Incubation of their starups with GEU-TBI. TBI is the Technology Business Incubator which is a government organization who promotes the startups in the country and universities.

Business Events

Udaan organizes a lot of business events so that students who wwant to become entrepreneurs and start their own startups and businesses can get an idea about how things work in real.
